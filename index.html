About me

My name is Szymon Gustav Snoeck, a third year applied math major at Columbia. My main research interests are machine learning and theoretical computer science problems which involve high-dimensional geometry or graph theory. However through graduate classes and independent work, I have also done research in algorithmic analysis, matching theory, dimension-reduction, and derandomization. Some of the work I have produced can be found below.
